Scrappie

Scrappie is a recipe scrapping webapp where the user can either web scrape a recipe online or upload their own recipe to the database. Because of the name and the inspiration for it, I decided to have an overarching design theme of arts and crafts and scrapbook.

To do app

As the main usage of a to do app is to provide a space to portray tasks to be done, I want to champion the tasks themselves. Hence I opted for a minimalistic art direction with a strong emphasis on the typography and limited color highlights.
